About Me

Who am I

I am a 29-year-old guy who is very interested about anything related to computers and computing technology.

I studied mechanical engineering in the University of Oulu in 2015-2021, but I discovered my true passion in computer science around 2017. Before that I was interested about computers at some level but university course about working principle of 8-bit computer lit truly my passion.

Fortunately computer science is very tightly integrated to mechanical engineering and I have been able to study IT stuff along side university studies of mechanical engineering and thus gathered pretty wide profession.

2021 I wrote a master's thesis which was about developing a computer vision system.

I want computing technology to be my main area of focus in the future as well, but I would be happy to avail my mechanical engineering skills combined with computing technology.

As an employee

I am entrepreneur-type, efficient and I like to be working with something constantly. I can work well all by myself or as part of the team. If needed I can take lead in a small group.

As a person I am calm and kind and I am able to work with all kinds of people.
